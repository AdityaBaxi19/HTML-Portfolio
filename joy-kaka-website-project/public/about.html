About Mr Joy Baxi

Mr. Joy Baxi is a highly experienced Chartered Accountant and a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), with over 25 years of extensive experience in the field of finance, accounting, and business advisory.

He qualified as a Chartered Accountant in 1991 and has since built a strong reputation for delivering strategic financial solutions to a wide range of clients, including corporates, SMEs, and startups.

Before establishing his practice, Mr. Baxi held key leadership positions in reputed organizations, serving as General Manager (Finance) and Chief Financial Officer (CFO) for India and South Asia operations. His corporate exposure has enabled him to develop deep insights into financial management, regulatory compliance, and business strategy.

Professional Expertise

Joy Baxi specializes in:

  • Financial Planning and Analysis
  • Strategic Business Advisory
  • Loan Syndication and Fund Raising
  • Cost Optimization and Profit Improvement
  • Business Restructuring and Turnaround Strategies
  • Internal Audit and Risk Management

About the Firm

Mr. Baxi is the founder of Joy Baxi & Associates, a Chartered Accountant firm established in 1996 and based in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. The firm has grown steadily over the years and is supported by a team of experienced professionals and partners.
